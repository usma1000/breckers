WHO WE ARE

Breckers Cutting Tools was established in 1953 and together, our team has over 250 years of cutting tool manufacturing experience. Our team is what makes us who we are and they are the reason you will find that doing business with us is different than any other cutting tool manufacturer in the industry. We built our company on four core values: tenacity, sense of urgency, grow or die, and servant leadership. We do business the right way, every time. Oh, and we make really good cutting tools.

DRILLS
Breckers drills are always made from the highest quality of carbide. Our state-of-the-art OD department and cutter grind department have the ability to hold the tightest of tolerances. Every drill is specifically designed for optimum cutting geometry, body design, and finish treatments for each customer’s specific application.
ENDMILLS
Breckers endmills are each uniquely designed for specific applications. They are designed to have a high rate of material removal and to increase tool life. Each material and application calls for a different design and Breckers endmills fit those needs.
REAMERS
It’s all in the details. When reaming with a Breckers reamer, details such as edge prep, flute finishings, and our inspection processes will make your job easier. With a Breckers reamer you can count on what’s important: repeatability, consistency, and taking tight tolerances to the extreme.
EMERGENCIES
Breckers does emergencies better than anyone else in the cutting tool industry. We understand that sometimes machines go down because of tooling. We are here to bring your machines back online faster than anyone else can.
